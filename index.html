What is CRM?

CRM is a customer relationship management software that stores customer details & logs customer activities throughout their journey with the company.

What is Kondesk?

Kondesk is a leading CRM software that allows users to store and utilize vital customer information, this will assist in delivering a seamless customer experience. However, it is not just a CRM platform, it is a business management platform targeted especially towards Education and Migration Industry.

Power or Kondesk?

Kondesk is an innovative platform which provide a 360* experience to the business and its customer. It assists businesses in managing their employees, HR activities, accounting activities, etc. It also assists businesses in providing an excellent customer experience by creating leads and storing valuable information to provide an excellent customer service.
